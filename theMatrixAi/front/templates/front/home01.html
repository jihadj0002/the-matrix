Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the product and billing.

Do AI replies and comment replies count toward my message limit?
Yes, only AI replies (both direct and suggestive) and comment automation messages count toward your monthly message quota.
Can I purchase additional messages if I reach my limit?
Yes, you can easily purchase additional messages whenever you exceed your plan limit. Each pack of 1,000 extra messages is available for just $5, with no disruption to your service.
Is there a mobile omnichannel inbox app available?
Yes, MatrixAi has a mobile app available on both the Play Store and App Store for managing chats on the go.
How do I connect my product inventory to MatrixAi?
You can connect your Shopify or WooCommerce store in one click. Custom website integrations may involve additional setup fees. Even you can use our MatrixAi product and order inventory in one place.
How is my customer data stored and protected?
We use isolated AWS S3 buckets and fully comply with Facebook, WhatsApp, and Instagram data policies to ensure your data is secure.
